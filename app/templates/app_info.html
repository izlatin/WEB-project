Here you can find useful information and tips!

  1. Creating a post
    To create a post you have be authorized.
    Use 'create a post' button to create a post - all fields have to be filled, one image is minimum.
    No images - no posts!
  2. Editing a post
    You can delete and edit your post:
    • Add or remove images
    • Edit text fields such as title, description and tags
    • You will NOT able to return back your post after deleting
  3. Commenting
    Click the title of a post and post comment - anyone will be able to see it!
    Editing and deleting comments is also possible.
  4. Replying
    The most interesting and useful
    • If want to propose a barter to someone's post - click "Reply" button
    • Replies to YOUR post you can see at your post's page (button "see replies")
    • If your barter is done, you got your wares - click the 'Done' button
      to add your post to archive
  5. Your info
    • Access your profile, where you can edit your data, by clicking "Profile" in menu
    • All your posts are placed in your profile, use "My posts" in menu
    • Archived posts are in "Archive" also in menu
  6. Search
    Use "search" field at the top of the site, we'll try to find your query
  7. API
    • Search post by id → /api/post/<id>
    • Search comments by post_id → /api/comments/<post_id>
    • Search posts by string → /api/search/<string> (To use different words use _ as delimiter)
